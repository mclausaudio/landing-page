    About

    Hello, my name is Michael. I'm a professional sound designer, audio engineer, and self-taught software developer from San Francisco, California. I began teaching myself JavaScript in 2014 and continue to use it as my primary programing language. You can check out some of my projects below. I'm also a published musician. I've released multiple 12" records, full length LPs and EPs. I co-run the label Outpost SF with my good friend Cassidy Martin and have my own label called Silva Electronics.
