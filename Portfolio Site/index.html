    About

    Hi! I'm Michael. I'm a professional sound designer, audio engineer, and self-taught software developer from San Francisco, California. I began teaching myself JavaScript in 2015 and continue to use it as my primary programing language. Currently, I write JavaScript on both the front-end and back-end using a variety of JS frameworks, NPM packages, and front-end libraries. I'm also a published musician. I've released multiple 12" records, full length LPs and EPs. I co-run the label Outpost SF with my good friend Cassidy Martin and have my own label called Silva Electronics.
